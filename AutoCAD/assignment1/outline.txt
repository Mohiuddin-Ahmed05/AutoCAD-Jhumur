4. KEY CONCEPTS / FORMULAS (reference)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
  - Azimuth (whole-circle bearing): angle 0-360 clockwise from North.
  - Quadrant bearing: N/S <angle> E/W. Conversions from azimuth (Az):
        NE quad (0-90)     : bearing = N (Az) E
        SE quad (90-180)   : bearing = S (180-Az) E
        SW quad (180-270)  : bearing = S (Az-180) W
        NW quad (270-360)  : bearing = N (360-Az) W
  - Closed traverse: the legs return to the start; for Fig 11-4(e) the single
    unknown leg AB is found graphically by closing the figure in AutoCAD.
  - Area of a closed polyline read directly from AutoCAD (no manual coordinate
    method needed, though it can be cross-checked).
